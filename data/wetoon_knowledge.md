## Instructional Claims To Reuse

- Wetoon is strongest when students start from their own idea, then let AI assist with scenario, character, cut, and revision steps.
- The educational value is not only the final comic but the loop of planning, prompting, reviewing, editing, and comparing outputs.
- Teacher-facing selling points include:
  - Korean-language workflow
  - tangible student outputs
  - classroom monitoring / classroom-sharing orientation
  - safety-sensitive filtering and teacher oversight

## Teacher-Facing Framing

- Elementary:
  - story order, empathy, safety, everyday dialogue, expression of feelings
- Middle school:
  - viewpoint, social issues, ethics, media literacy, prompt precision
- High school:
  - rhetoric, criticism, career education, issue interpretation, project-based outputs

## Wetoon Features To Mention In Suggestions

- Style selection
- Plot writing and chatbot support
- Character generation with descriptive details
- Scenario selection and editing
- Cut revision and additional cut generation
- Speech bubbles, sound effects, text editing
- Title finalization and download
- Optional expansion to video/animation
